Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Why stories?

A: Story-telling is one of the oldest forms of entertainment. Whether fictional or not, we tell stories everyday to our children, our friends, our families. Stories are fun!


Q: How does Flomad work?

A: It's simple. Take a turn writing a few words, and then watch as the the next user, and the user, add on to what you've written. Gradually, a story will develop. You can do this with your friend sitting next to you, or all the way across the country


Q: Why did you create Flomad?

A: Most games out there today require very little brainpower. We wanted to make something that challenges a player's creativity and is still a lot of fun.


Q: Who is Flomad for?

A: There is no specific age limit or range for Flomad. We encourage all to try it out - kids and adults, couples and families.


Q: How much does Flomad cost to use?

A: Reading, writing, and sharing stories on Flomad is free for all users.


Q: Why can I only input 120 characters?

So everyone else gets a chance to add to the story as well!


Go ahead. Be creative.
